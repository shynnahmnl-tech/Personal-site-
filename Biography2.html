About Me

I’m from Iligan City, Lanao del Norte. I’m currently 14 years old and will be turning 15 this July.

I study at St. Therese Academy. I’m passionate about many things, including coding, reading manhwa, playing with my cats, learning about history, and biking.

Coding is especially exciting for me because I get to create things like this website and bring my ideas to life. It allows me to turn my thoughts into functional and creative projects, which is a feeling that never gets old.

I am also very proud to share that I have won second place in a coding competition. It was a wonderful experience that challenged me and showed me how much I have grown in my skills. It motivates me to keep learning and doing even better in the future.

I was born in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, and my favorite color is green—which you can probably see reflected in my website's design!

Interests & Hobbies

In my free time, I love doing things that bring me joy and help me grow as a person. Reading manhwa, coding, playing with my cats, learning about history, and biking are some of my favorite activities. Each of these hobbies holds a special place in my heart, and they all contribute to making my life more meaningful and fulfilling.

Reading manhwa is one of my greatest passions. It takes me to new worlds, introduces me to interesting characters, and expands my imagination in ways that nothing else can. Whether I am reading a fictional manhwa story or a non-fiction book about real events, I always find myself learning something new and gaining a different perspective on life. It is a wonderful way to relax and escape from the busy world around me, while also keeping my mind active and curious.

Coding is another hobby that I am deeply passionate about. As I mentioned earlier, it allows me to create things from scratch and bring my ideas to life. It challenges me to think creatively, solve problems, and pay attention to small details. Every time I write a piece of code and see it work, I feel a great sense of accomplishment and pride. It is a skill that I am constantly working on improving, and I know that it will be valuable to me in the future. Winning second place in a coding competition was a huge moment for me, and it proved to myself that I can achieve great things through hard work and dedication.

Spending time with my cats is something that I look forward to every day. They are such lovely and affectionate creatures, and they always know how to make me smile. Playing with them, cuddling with them, and watching them grow up are some of the happiest moments of my life. They bring so much warmth and laughter into my home, and I am so grateful to have them in my life.
